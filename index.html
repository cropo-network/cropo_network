Our Vision
Helping individuals manage and benefit their data

Despite existing regulations like GDPR, data remains a game dominated by big companies, leaving the real owners and value creators ‒ individuals ‒ out of the picture. CroPo, short for Crowd Power, aims to change this landscape by giving everyone the power to participate and be rewarded. Here's how:

Crowd Source:

Create Data: Individuals can contribute data for crowdsourced tasks.

Annotate Data: Qualified individuals can annotate data as requested.

Consent Management:

Manage Data: Our data management tool helps individuals collect and authorize their data across various apps according to their preferences.

Sell Data: On our trading platform, individuals can set a price for their data, allowing verified companies to rent or bid for it, similar to an NFT platform.

Highlights

Lower transaction costs and barriers

By utilizing ZK networks and KYC solutions, a trustworthy and convenient identity authentication system and low-cost labor payments are achieved.

Efficient capital utilization

Offering a dual-mode of project tokens or USDT for clients to choose from, workers can receive their wages instantly while clients can pay the final payment after project acceptance.

Decentralized identity system at the contract layer

Workers can purchase new identities to access more tasks, accumulate experience to upgrade their identities, and receive more task rewards, enhancing user engagement on the platform.

Trustworthy anonymous inspection and penalty mechanism

Authorized anonymous workers or client roles on the chain will conduct cross-checks at various stages, supplemented by downgrade penalty contracts, to ensure data and annotation results meet task requirements as much as possible.

Data copyright traceability and protection

By adopting decentralized data authentication and traceability technologies, the credibility of worker data sources and customer data ownership are maximally ensured.

User data authorization transactions

Using ZKML technology, it is possible to realize data valuation and data transactions on the chain without compromising user privacy.

How does Crowd Power create datasets?

Unprocessed and scattered raw data from the crowd holds limited value. Crowd Powerharnesses distributed data and workforces to collaboratively generate highly valuable datasets.

Collection
Contributors gather and upload raw data based on specific dataset requirements, ensuring an inclusive process with no participation barriers.
Annotation
Expert contributors clean and label raw data, making it comprehensible for AI learning.
Inspection
To guarantee data quality, both raw and annotated data undergo a thorough review by verified contributors.
Licensing
The finalized datasets can be licensed to multiple clients, generating ongoing revenue shared among all contributors.
